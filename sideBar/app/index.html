This is demo of sidemenu implementation.

This demo include 2 types of sidemenu.
First sidemenu is for PC. if you widen screen size of browser over 961px of width, blue side menu will appear. This is normal sidemenu that means no resizing and no dinamics.
Second sidemenu is for mobile as tablet or smartphone. if you shrink screen size under 960px of width, gray 3 lines will appear on the header. If you click that, blue side menu will appear.
The second type of sidemenu is more complex than first one. That is implemented by @media tag , transform attribute + toggleClass method of jQuery.

I think this design is too cheap to show others, but it worth good experience for me that show on web.
